# SITE.md — זמני כניסת ויציאת שבת בירוחם

> Site vision, Stitch project reference, sitemap, and roadmap for the Build Loop.

## 1. Vision

A calm, beautiful Hebrew (RTL) site that shows **Shabbat times for Yerucham (ירוחם)** —
candle-lighting time (**זמן הדלקת נרות / כניסת שבת**) and Shabbat end / Havdalah
(**צאת השבת / יציאת שבת**). The experience should feel serene and a little sacred:
an evening sky fading into night, warm candlelight, generous whitespace, and large,
legible Hebrew typography. Primary use is "glance and know" — a visitor lands and
immediately sees this week's entry and exit times for Yerucham.

## 2. Audience

- Residents and visitors of Yerucham who want this week's Shabbat times.
- Hebrew-first speakers; layout is **RTL** by default.
- Mobile-first (most people check on a phone Friday afternoon), but desktop must look intentional.
